Local Storage Utilities

All financial information is stored entirely inside your device browser's persistent storage engine (IndexedDB). Your data is 100% private. To back up your data or switch devices, click Export Backup to download a file. Import it anytime to restore your history.

How to Save and Launch Locally

  1. Push the file changes to your **GitHub** repository.
  2. Open your secure GitHub Pages link in **Safari** on your iPhone.
  3. Tap the **Share** button in Safari and choose **Add to Home Screen** to run it natively.
  4. Your device will automatically persist your transactions even when you close the browser window.

Danger Zone

This resets your local configuration back to default settings. Ensure you have exported a JSON backup first if you want to keep your historical purchases.
